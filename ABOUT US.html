ABOUT DESK

Business space facilitator and revenue generator for barren, under-utilised or non revenue generating property.

Desk aims to build a business space facilitating and management website, which will enable the property owners to list their barren, under-utilized or non-revenue generating property spaces and generate revenue by converting such spaces into an ideal working setup.

By providing these spaces to entrepreneurs, freelancers or just any client who requires flexible working spaces, these such properties will gradually generate revenue and will become a profitable investment for their owners.

Desk bridges the gap and allows the clients to list their requirements on the website, while property owners can list their properties and cater to the requirement(s).
